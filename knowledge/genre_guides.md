# Genre and vibe guide

pop: bright hooks, high valence, dance friendly.
rock: stronger guitars, medium-high energy, often intense or rebellious.
lofi: low energy, mellow, often instrumental, good for focus.
edm: very high energy and tempo, dance-driven, strong drops.
jazz: groove and harmony, can be calm or exploratory.
ambient: texture-heavy, very calm, low tempo and low energy.
hip-hop: rhythm-first, confident or dark moods, strong groove.
afrobeats: upbeat percussion, high danceability, joyful energy.
metal: very high energy and aggression, intense emotional tone.

Vibe heuristics:
- gym vibe: high energy and high tempo.
- focus vibe: low-medium energy and higher acousticness.
- party vibe: high danceability and high valence.
- wind-down vibe: low energy, calm/peaceful mood.
